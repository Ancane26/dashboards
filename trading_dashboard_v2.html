Strategy Performance

Equity Curve — Cumulative R

Goal: rising curve = the system is generating edge. Shape reveals acceleration vs plateau. X-axis = trade number, not time — removes the distortion of trade frequency.

Drawdown — Underwater Curve

Goal: how far below peak equity are you at any point? Shallower + faster recovery = better risk-adjusted performance. Deep flat drawdowns signal strategy stress.

Score vs R-Outcome — Signal Quality

Goal: validate that higher scores produce better outcomes. Green = wins, red = losses. A right-skewed green cluster (high score → positive R) confirms the scoring model has edge. No pattern = score is noise.

Win R Distribution — when the trade works, by how much?

Goal: understand the shape of your wins. Losses are always −1R (fixed stop), so the useful question is: are winning trades hitting 2R+? Left-skewed wins (mostly +1R) mean your targets or entries need work.

Setup Performance — Expectancy by Setup

Goal: identify which setups carry the strategy vs drag it. Kill or paper-trade losers; size up on winners once the sample is large enough (n ≥ 20 closed).

Monthly P&L

Goal: spot calendar patterns and regime shifts. Consecutive red months signal strategy stress or market condition mismatch.

Sector Relative Rotation — RRG

Goal: see which sectors are Leading (strong + accelerating), Weakening (strong but slowing), Lagging (weak + falling), or Improving (weak but recovering). Trade Leading; avoid Lagging.
